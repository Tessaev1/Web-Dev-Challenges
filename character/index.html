Dwight Schrute is a star character in the hit series, The Office, portrayed by Rainn Wilson. As a paper salesman and Assistant to the Regional Manager at Dunder Mifflin, Dwight prides himself in his work and loyalty to the failing paper company. Though diligent and dedicated, Dwight is also very naive and is often pranked by his deskmate Jim Halpert.
